[
Emma Watson is sitting on the floor in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rabin_Square">Rabin Square</a>

and some bystanders listening or

]

[

—
<img src="./images/emma-watson-wandless.svg.webp" alt="Emma Watson" title="Emma Watson played Hermione in the Harry Potter films but she doesn't need a wand to kick your ass" />

]

Emma Watson: and so Simba and Nala ruled over the Pride Lands with the
help of Rafiki, Timon and Pumba. And
<a href="https://mlp.fandom.com/wiki/Discord">Discord</a>
and <a href="https://mlp.fandom.com/wiki/Pinkie_Pie">Pinkie Pie</a>
made sure life in the Pride Lands will have its share of
non-mundane excitement. And <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moses">Moses</a>
and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judas_Maccabeus">Yehudah Hamakabi</a>
trained both the antelopes and the lions to run faster and faster.
And <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/bits/facts/Xena/">Xena the
Warrior Princess</a> made sure Moses and Yehudah can coexist at the same
time.

+++: And they all lived happily ever after. The End.

[ Applause. ]

Emma Watson: Well, this isn't exactly how the original
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Lion_King">"Lion King"</a> film went,
but we're allowed to improvise, aren't we?

+++: Hmmm... it is now [she looks at her watch] 10 A.M. so we've got time until
lunch, and I'm kind-of hungry.

The children: Yeah, we're hungry too.

Emma Watson: OK, would you like some icecream?

The children: yeah... all right...

Emma Watson: let's go then.

<s id="emma-watson-and-the-children-get-icecream" title="Getting icecream">

[The kids and Emma enter <a href="https://www.vaniglia.co.il/">Vaniglia</a>
at Ibn Gabirol st. There are two vendors - Ronen, a male in his 20s to the right,
and Efrat, an 18 years old female.]

Ronen: Shalom...

Emma: uvrakhah [she winks] but my Hebrew is quite rudimentary, so let's speak English.

Ronen: oh sure, welcome to Vaniglia, I am Ronen, and this is Efrat.

Emma: Pleasure to meet you. I'm Emma.

+++: [to the children] OK, I know you kids can get indecisive, so please
don't waste too much time on choosing flavours. They are extremely unlikely
to be poisonous.

A kid: sure!

[They go to order. Emma heads towards Efrat.]

Emma: Okay, can I leave a tip using my credit card?

Efrat: sorry! It is not possible.

Emma: in that case [she pulls out her wallet] take these four 100 Sheqel.
And keep the change as tip.

Efrat: wow, this is a lot to pay, Emma…

Emma: Watson! Emma Watson! I would have thought the shirt is a dead give away,
but I guess <a href="https://www.joelonsoftware.com/2000/04/26/designing-for-people-who-have-better-things-to-do-with-their-lives/">one
cannot expect people to read</a>.

Efrat: [looks at Emma 's shirt and name tag] Oh my god! You're the Emma Watson!
This is so cool! I'm too young to have watched the Harry Potter films,
but I loved you on <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/Selina-Mandrake/">Selina Mandrake</a>. I even donated 20 dollars for the pledge to make
it <a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/">CC-by</a>. My
father donated even more.

Emma: thanks, that was generous of you.

Ronen: Emma, so what shall I give you?

Emma: Mango, chocolate, and lemon - in a cone - with the lemon scoop at the bottom.

Ronen: no problem, but we have dark chocolate, orange…

Emma: dark chocolate!

Ronen: coming right up…

[Cut to Efrat.]

Efrat: OK, here is the change and the receipt.

Emma: Thanks! [puts the change in the tips' glass jar.]

Efrat: So, how do you like it here?

Emma: Frankly speaking, it does not feel much different from Greater London.
It is warmer, and there is a wider selection of food, and the Hebrew
and stuff, but… feels right at home.

Efrat: And what's the deal with these kids?

Emma: Oh that… Well, their parents approached me in Olamot con, while I
was chatting with some other attendees and they told me they were looking
for someone to babysit them until about 1 o'clockish. I told them I'd do it
(because I always <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/Selina-Mandrake/ongoing-text.html#getting-help-with-cuneiform">try to help others</a>)
and they felt they can trust me.

+++: So I took these children on a walk and then told them our own variation
of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Lion_King">The Lion King</a>
and now I'm buying them icecream. They are sweet kids and they have only talked
in English in my presence so I'd be able to understand what they say.

Efrat: Sounds great!

Emma: Now what about you?

Efrat: Well, I'm in the twelfth grade now, and planning to be a software
dev in the army… I mean the military. I'm good at maths and been programming
since the third grade, and the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel_Defense_Forces">IDF</a> is desperate for software devs and
it's something I do enjoy.

Emma: Good luck, well, I'd better eat my icecream, here is my card - you can
IM me when your shift's over and we can meet somewhere near the con.

+++: … oh wait, I nearly forgot - would you two like to record a video with me?

Ronen: Yes, please, here - you can use my phone.

[One of the children takes the phone and records.]

Ronen: Hi, I'm Ronen!

Efrat: I'm Efrat!

Emma: And I'm Emma Watson.
<a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/Selina-Mandrake/ongoing-text.html#selina-and-the-three">“Mighty Klingon vampire warriors who have watched Sesame Street… this decade royally sucks!!”</a>

[They all laugh. The child stops recording and returns the phone.]

Emma: Thank you, and good bye.

Ronen: You’re welcome! And thanks!

[Cut.]

<s id="emulating-arnie-as-hamlet" title="Emulating Arnold Schwarzenegger as Hamlet">

[

[
Two children emulating <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9Eont_yEGZs">the Hamlet parody scene</a>
: a boy playing Schwarzenegger and a girl doing the special effects and the narration.

+++: … and — Hamlet is taking out the trash.

Boy: Stay thy hand fair prince.

+++: who says I'm fair?

Girl: [machine gun firing sounds]

Boy: To be or not to be…

Girl: [explosion sounds]

<s id="emma-and-arnold-meet-aharon" title="Emma and Arnold meet Aharon">

Arnold: Ms. Watson, I presume!

Emma: [laughs] May the Schwarzenegger be with you!

Arnold: Heh, some jokes never die.

Emma: At least not until <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/fortunes/show.cgi?id=shlomif-fact-chuck-140">Chuck Norris gets to taking them out of their misery</a>.

+++: Anyway, let's get to business. Do we wait for our terminators?

Arnold: oh look [he points at Aharon the good terminator.]

Aharon: Mr. Schwarzenegger, Ms. Watson, I am Aharon. Please follow me.

Emma: Yes, sir.

[ They walk]

Aharon: For your information, Hannah, Emma's lookalike evil terminator was sent
here to kill you both.

Arnold: sounds like it will make a good plot device. "Ask not what your profits
should do for you. Ask what you should do for your profits." - a
<a href="https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Rules_of_Acquisition">Ferengi acquisition</a> rule.

<s id="fighting-the-evil-terminator" title="Fighting the evil terminator">

<s id="where-is-hannah" title="Where is Hannah">

Emma: So, Aharon, where is this evil Hannah terminator anyway?

Aharon: over there - she is busy being an <a href="https://tvtropes.org/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/AttentionWhore">attention whore</a>.

[ They see Hannah through the window talking, interviewing, and posing for her photos with fans. ]

Arnold: looks like it will take a while - can we escape?

Aharon: there is no escape from destiny. But we can speed the start of the battle up
by using a time wrapped game level.

+++: Would that be acceptable?

Arnold: I guess - might as well get it over with now.

Emma: yes... Please <a href="https://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/9695/whats-the-origin-of-picards-signature-phrase-make-it-so">make it so</a>.

Aharon: On it.

<s id="hannah-using-machine-gun" title="Hannah using machine gun">

[ Hannah storms towards the good guys' trio, who are hiding behind a bunker,
while spray shooting. ]

Aharon: This is too easy! Initiate electromagnet.

[ An upper electromagnet attracts Hannah's AK-47 to the ceiling
and she tries in vein to release it. ]

Aharon: Initiate supersonic storm.

[ <a href="https://mlp.fandom.com/wiki/Rainbow_Dash">Rainbow Dash</a>
sets up a cyclone which twirls Hannah out of the machine gun's reach. ]

Aharon: eject AK-47!

[ The AK-47 is thrown out of one of the hall's upper windows.
]

</s>

<s id="hannah-using-a-tank" title="Hannah using a tank">

Message on the screen: Level 2: Tank!

[ Hannah is driving a tank with the newfangled anti-
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RPG-7">RPG</a>
receptors. ]

Emma: oh my god! This is kinda thrilling in a way.

Arnold: Can't we catapult many wooden logs to destroy these
anti-RPG thingies?

Aharon: good call, Mr. Schwarzenegger! Initiate anti-anti-anti-tank.

[ A flood of wooden logs lands on or around the tank devastating the
anti-RPG weapons and shaking the tank. ]

Hannah: bloody fuck!

Aharon: Initiate anti-tank.

[ <a href="https://mlp.fandom.com/wiki/Fluttershy">Fluttershy</a> emerges
from the mist, mounts an <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RPG-7">RPG-7</a>, aims, fires, and destroys Hannah's tank. ]

Fluttershy: hasta la vista, baby tank girl!

[ Inspired by
<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dIeuBPDUzB0">Terminator vs Jesus HD The Greatest Action Story Ever Told Mad T.V. 1996</a>
, by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tank_Girl">Tank Girl</a>
and by Emma Watson and Hannah being short.
]

Rainbow Dash: that was 101% awesome Flutteroo!

Arnold: yeah!

Emma: smashing!

</s>

Hannah: Sounds good!

[

Message on the screen: Battle won! Hannah has lost.

]

Aharon: Initiate "Exit game".
